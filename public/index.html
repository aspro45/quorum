How it works

Three steps, zero gatekeepers.

01

Fund the treasury

Anyone tops up the shared pool with GEN. The balance is transparent and on-chain; every change is visible.

02

Request a grant

A builder submits a title, a plan, and an amount. It enters the queue, public from the first block.

03

The rubric decides

The contract reads the plan against the rubric and validators agree. Merits funding: paid instantly. Does not: rejected, with a reason.
